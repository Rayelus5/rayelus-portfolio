Vision & About


About us

We are a company specialising in web and graphic design, as well as renovating and creating brand images from scratch for companies or individuals. With more than 5 years of experience. Minimal Web was created and founded by Rayelus in 2022.

We work closely with content creators on Youtube, Instagram, X (Twitter), Twitch, and with companies. Since we started we have received very positive feedback.

Our mission

What characterises us in this sector is the Prestige, Originality and Professionalism of our work. Always offering Professional and Quality packs. We always have a direct contact with our customers, allowing us greater transparency and productivity in our work.

We offer a personalised service depending on the client, including revisions depending on the client's needs.
